Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products

What makes your plywood different from others?

Our plywood is made from 100% Gurjan hardwood face with ISI certified manufacturing processes. We use premium adhesives that make our products termite-proof, warp-resistant and moisture-proof, ensuring longer lifespan.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes, we offer special pricing for bulk orders. The discount percentage depends on the order quantity and product type. Please contact our sales team for a customized quote.

What are your delivery timelines?

For orders within Jabalpur, we deliver within 24-48 hours. For other locations in Madhya Pradesh, delivery takes 3-5 days. Pan-India deliveries typically take 5-7 working days depending on the destination.

Can I get custom sizes for moulding beet?

Yes, we specialize in custom-sized moulding beet for specific project requirements. Minimum order quantities apply for custom sizes. Share your requirements with our team for a quote.

How do I place an order?

You can place orders through WhatsApp (9300780406), by calling us, or by filling out the contact form on this website. Our team will confirm your order and provide payment details.
